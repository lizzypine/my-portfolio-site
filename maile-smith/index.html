A little bit about me.

I have always had a passion for listening to and telling stories. I believe a good story gets people to listen in a way that facts and opinions can't. I also believe there is a story behind everything.

I think stories can be found in the most unassuming places and that a good story makes your product or business more compelling. If you're not sure how to tell your story, I'm the person for the job.

Online Formatting

Writing for online is significantly different than writing for print. I know how to focus on the nuances of hooks, length, and other factors to keep your readers engaged.

Social Media Strategy

I work with businesses to develop their social media presence online, including writing for SEO.

Editing and Proofreading

You can count on me to produce quality, error-free writing. Having served as a newspaper editor, I am skilled at proofreading others' work and specialize in telling the stories of those who aren't natural authors.
